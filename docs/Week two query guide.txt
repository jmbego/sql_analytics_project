

Business Questions & Query Guidelines

1. Customer Segmentation Analysis
Business Question: Who are our most valuable customer segments by demographic and geographic factors?

2. Product Performance Analysis
Business Question: Which products and categories drive the most revenue and have the best inventory turnover?

3. Sales Channel Effectiveness
Business Question: Which traffic sources and geographic regions drive the most valuable customers?

4. Customer Behavior Analysis
Business Question: How do customer engagement patterns correlate with purchasing behavior?

5. Time-Based Sales Analysis
Business Question: How do sales patterns vary by time periods and seasons?
